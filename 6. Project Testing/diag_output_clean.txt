Data Logic Analysis:
- In this dataset, raw DAYS_EMPLOYED=365243 represents pensioners.
- preprocess.py isolates this anomaly via: app_df['DAYS_EMPLOYED'].replace(365243, 0)
- It then safely transforms it: YEARS_EMPLOYED = np.abs(DAYS_EMPLOYED) / 365.25
- Therefore, in our model, BOTH 'Pensioners' and genuine 'Unemployed' hit exactly YEARS_EMPLOYED=0.

- To distinguish them, the algorithm natively interacts YEARS_EMPLOYED with AGE and NAME_INCOME_TYPE in the Random Forest.
